## Overview  

**AirBuddy for macOS** brings the smooth, intuitive AirPods experience from iPhone to your Mac — enabling instant connections, precise battery tracking, and fast switching between all your Apple devices. Designed to feel native to macOS, **AirBuddy 2** integrates deeply with your system to make connecting and managing Bluetooth audio effortless.  

When you open your AirPods case near your Mac, AirBuddy instantly detects them and displays a beautiful pop-up showing each component’s battery level. You can connect with one click, just like on iOS. The app supports AirPods, Beats, and other Bluetooth audio devices, ensuring complete compatibility across your ecosystem.  

Beyond basic connectivity, **AirBuddy** adds automation and customization that power users will love. You can set smart connection preferences for specific devices, manage multiple outputs, and control when your AirPods automatically switch between Mac and iPhone. Integration with **macOS Shortcuts** lets you build powerful Bluetooth automations, from triggering connection sequences to managing audio routing during workflows.  

AirBuddy also features **Magic Handoff**, a seamless way to transfer audio sessions between devices without delay. It’s lightweight, fast, and built for performance — optimized for **M1, M2, and M3 Macs**, ensuring flawless pairing and minimal system resource usage.  

If you value convenience, design precision, and a truly Apple-like experience, **AirBuddy for Mac** is the ultimate companion for managing your wireless devices with style and simplicity.
